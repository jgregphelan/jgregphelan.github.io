Profile

My career has spanned software engineering at Bell Labs, founding and leading a technology consulting firm, serving as Acting CTO of a global nonprofit, teaching writing and technology at the university level, and coaching hundreds of writers through Project Write Now and book inc.

Across these roles, my work has centered on a consistent challenge: helping people with specialized knowledge communicate clearly to broader audiences. Whether collaborating with engineers, executives, entrepreneurs, educators, or writers, I help shape complex material into language that preserves rigor, nuance, and voice.
